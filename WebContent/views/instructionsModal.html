Game intructions

Internet plays game is a multiplayer PVP game. You enter the game by simply going to the URL in the browser. Once the page has loaded, you receive a unique player indentity. That identity is used to give you credit for the points you gain or lose.

You only get to see the actual state of the game after a successful action (move or reveal). Actions take resources and you get 1 resource per second.

Objective

Score points by steping on the yellow blob. Avoid steping on the red blob, this resets all your points.

Controlls

Move

Arrow keys or W, A, S, D (Requires 8 resources)

Reveal

Spacebar (Requires 2 resources)

Misc

You will not be able to retrieve the same user if you leave the page.
You will be kicked from the game if you are AFK for more than 1 minute.
